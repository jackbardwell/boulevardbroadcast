Boulevard Broadcast

Boulevard Broadcast, a mobile radio station that has the unique ability to broadcast signals between the past and the future of Milton Keynes. Cycling around A Festival of Creative Urban Living you will find No Purpose Collective gathering the sounds and stories of the city, discovering what it means to live in Milton Keynes today and in the Future.
